Bill Gates's Principal Works

  • 1972 - Co-founds a company called Traf-O-Data, which analyzes local traffic patterns.
  • 1974 - Bill Gates and Paul Allen formed Microsoft.
  • 1976 - Gates wrote his famous "Open Letter to Hobbyists", accusing them of software piracy.
  • 1980 - Develops MS-DOS (Microsoft Disk Operating System) for IBM.
  • 1980 - Begins developing and promoting CD-ROM technology
  • 1989 - Founder of Corbis, a digital image company.
  • 2000 - Founds Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
  • 2004 to present - Concentrate on philanthropy, doing investments to some researchs.
